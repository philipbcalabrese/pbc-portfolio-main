Blackmores Lament

Unreal Engine

2024

During the 2024 spring quarter, I took Game Engines for Filmmakers which focused on utilizing Unreal Engine to create a short cinematic film. Inspired by fantasy worlds like Game of Thrones and Lord of the Rings, I built a medieval town encompassed by a thick forest and rolling hills. Through this project, I learned the fundamental skills of Unreal Engine and how to use it for cinematic purposes. This project specifically started my curiosity about the world where technology and art meet as before this I had primarily had a technical background.
